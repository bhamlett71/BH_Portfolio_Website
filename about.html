About

My Story

I'm a Bronx-born creator who loves people and loves to find the beauty in everything, even the cloudiest of days.

I'm a UX Researcher with more than six years researching and providing engaging user experiences in the education industry. I love discovering the way I can provide pleasurable solutions for real people with real problems, while collaborating with others. My goal is to continue contributing to users' experiences and inspire others to join UX! I also enjoy learning from others as well as mentoring and relationship-building.

When I'm not working, I love spending time with my family, friends, traveling, and exploring my city, NYC.
